South and Rucks

2017

A subscription box barber service I created and designed. The storyline and history is based off of my travel experiences at Grandad Bluff in La Crosse, WI and a historic barber shop located in the center of the town.

I really wanted to create a product that was more physical and an actual product design. I constructed the design around the launch phase of the subscription box so I could focus not only on the literal box designs but also promotional print and digital materials as well.
