Rēkən

2019

Yanqui is a fictional festival that honors not only the music but the attendants and personalities that can be found on the grounds. Celebrating everyone from or new to the U.S. who considers themselves to be an audiophile.

The main focus of yanqui was the app designed for the festival. Which was designed to be used by both the audience and everyone working the festival as well.
