Room

2016

An exhibition for Rhode Island based designers, Pneuhaus. ROOM was built around Pneuhaus’ study of sensations created when interacting with an object vs being contained by said object.

We decided to make the space and design seem barren and dark to accentuate the inflatable pieces. To add on to these design aspects, we used a mostly monochromatic color scheme and a variety of geometric shapes inspired by Pneuhaus’ pieces.

Designed in collaboration with Leslie E. Chung.
